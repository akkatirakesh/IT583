What is HTML5?

HTML5 is a natural evolution of earlier versions of HTML and strives to reflect the needs of both current and future Websites. It inherits the vast majority of features from its predecessors, meaning that if you coded HTML before HTML5 came on the scene, you already know a lot of HTML5.
